Copy trading is the process of buying and selling securities identical to those you own without actually owning them. This strategy can be profitable if the market moves in your favor, but it is also risky because you are not owning the assets you are trading. Copy trading may be ideal for investors who want to diversify their portfolio but don’t want to hold the securities themselves. Some investors prefer to use copy trading software to do the trade for them rather than dealing with the risk of dealing directly with a broker.
